# CUE FAQ

## Account
Q: I can't log in.
A: Check your email and password, then try "Forgot password". If it still fails, contact support.

## Bookings
Q: How does booking work?
A: Request → Accept → Secure payment → Event → Confirmation → Review.

## Payments
Q: My payment was declined.
A: Check 3D Secure, card limits, expiry date, then try again or use another card.
